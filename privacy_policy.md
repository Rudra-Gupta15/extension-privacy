# Privacy Policy for BalanceTab

**Effective Date:** March 22, 2026

BalanceTab ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in the BalanceTab Chrome extension.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

### 1.1 Geolocation Data
BalanceTab requests access to your location to provide accurate local weather information. We use geolocation to show local weather.
- **How it's used:** Your latitude and longitude are sent to the Open-Meteo API (https://open-meteo.com) and ipapi (https://ipapi.co) to retrieve weather data and city names.
- **Storage:** We do **not** store your coordinate data on any servers. It is used only for the immediate request.

### 1.2 Browser Data
BalanceTab uses the `tabs` and `sessions` permissions to display your recently closed and currently open tabs on the "Recent Tabs" panel.
- **Storage:** This data is processed locally within your browser and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

### 1.3 Personal Settings and Notes
User settings and notes created in the "Notebook" feature are stored locally on your device using `chrome.storage.local`.
- **Storage:** These are stored entirely within your browser's local storage and are never uploaded to any cloud service or server.

## 2. Third-Party Services
BalanceTab interacts with the following third-party services:
- **Open-Meteo API**: For weather data.
- **ipapi**: For IP-based location fallback.
- **Google Fonts**: For typography (Orbitron and Inter).
- **Search Engines**: When you perform a search, you are redirected to the selected search engine (Google, DuckDuckGo, Bing, etc.).
- **AI Services**: When using the AI Assistant feature, you are redirected to Claude.ai or other selected AI platforms. No data is sent to these services automatically; redirection only occurs when you click "Ask".

## 3. Data Security
Since BalanceTab does not collect or store personal data on any remote servers, the security of your data depends on the security of your local device and browser.

## 4. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the extension or its store listing.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store developer contact form.
