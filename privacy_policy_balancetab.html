Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

The BalanceTab — Gamer + Office extension is designed with privacy as a core principle. Here is how your data is handled:

Local Storage Only

All settings, notebook entries, and high scores created within this extension are stored locally on your device using the Chrome Storage API. No data is ever transmitted to, or stored on, our servers or any third-party servers.

Permissions & Usage

  • storage / unlimitedStorage: Used to save your settings, notebook entries, game high scores, and custom media (like video wallpapers) safely on your local machine.
  • tabs / sessions: Used to display your recently closed and open tabs within the dashboard widget.
  • favicon: Used to display website icons for your recent tabs.
  • geolocation: Used to fetch your local city and temperature via the Open-Meteo API.

    • No Tracking

    We do not use any tracking, analytics, or cookies. We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII).

    Third-Party Services

    BalanceTab interacts with the following services to provide its core features:

    • Open-Meteo API: For real-time weather data.
    • ipinfo.io: For location fallback if GPS is unavailable.
    • Google Fonts: To provide custom typography.

    Contact

    If you have any questions, please contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store developer contact form.
