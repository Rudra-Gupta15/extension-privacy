Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

The Growth Tracker — Productivity Tree extension is built on the foundation of user privacy and data ownership. Here is exactly how your information is handled:

Local Storage Policy

Every piece of data generated by this extension—including your site tracking history, focus session logs, todo lists, calendar reminders, project targets, and custom domain settings—is stored only on your local device using the Chrome Storage API.

We do not operate any backend servers. Your data never leaves your computer, ensuring you have 100% control over your productivity statistics.

Permissions & Transparency

  • storage: Used strictly to save your preferences and daily progress locally.
  • tabs / activeTab: Used to identify whether the current site should be tracked for growth or distraction. Tracking only occurs while Chrome is your active, focused window.
  • idle: Used to detect when you switch to another app or step away from your keyboard, allowing the extension to pause tracking automatically.

No Tracking & Analytics

We do not use tracking pixels, Google Analytics, or any form of cookies. We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) at any point during your usage.

Contact

Since this extension is client-side only, there are no accounts to manage and no personal data to delete. If you have any technical questions, please reach out via the Chrome Web Store support page.
