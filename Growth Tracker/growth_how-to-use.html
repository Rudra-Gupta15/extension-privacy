Choosing Your Paradise

Your productivity space should inspire you. Switch between four unique environments in Settings.

🌴

Oasis

The classic productivity experience. A lush coconut tree that thrives as you work. Perfect for a tropical work-from-home vibe.

🌵

Desert

Built for the resilient. A sharp cactus that blooms under the desert sun. Best for those who enjoy steady, unwavering progress.

🌸

Sakura

A tranquil cherry blossom tree. Productive sessions fill the branches with pink blossoms, while inactivity lets the petals fall softly.

🌙

Midnight

A dark-mode bioluminescent willow. Glowing neon vines pulse with light as you focus, turning your workspace into a tech-zen garden.

Zen Principle: Consistency

Building a paradise takes time. Your tree's health resets every 24 hours to give you a fresh start. If you step away from your keyboard, we'll automatically pause your timers so your data stays pure.
