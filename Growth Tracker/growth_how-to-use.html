Welcome to the Oasis

1
Configuration

Set Your Boundaries

Go to Settings and define your Growth and Neutral domains. Any website not listed will automatically be treated as a distraction.

2
Growth

Watch Your Paradise Bloom

Stay on productive sites to see your tree grow tall and lush. Inactivity or too much distraction will cause the leaves to wither and coconuts to fall.

3
Tasks

Cultivate Your Habits

Use the Todo tab to manage your daily recurring tasks. Mark them off to stay on track; daily tasks reset every morning to help you build consistency.

4
Focus

Deep Work Stopwatch

Use the Timer tab to track a specific project. Paste a link, start the timer, and we'll record exactly how much deep-work progress you made on that target.

5
Intelligence

YouTube Study Mode

We automatically detect educational content on YouTube based on video titles. You can also manually toggle Study Mode in the Timer tab to stay productive while learning.

6
Analysis

Review Your Journey

Check the Tracker for a detailed percentage breakdown of your day. Use the Calendar to mark milestones and journal your daily success.

7
Preferences

Personalize Your Oasis

Visit Settings to toggle Dark Mode (Oasis Dark) for a better nighttime experience, or reset your tracking data if you need a fresh start.

8
Important

Active Tracking Only

Time is only tracked while Chrome is your active, focused window. If you switch to another app, your desktop, or lock your screen, the timer automatically pauses.
