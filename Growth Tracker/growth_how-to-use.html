The Growth Engine

Your tree grows in real-time as you browse Productive sites. Watch it wilt or lose coconuts if you spend too much time on distractions.

Focus Engine

Choose between Stopwatch (track any project link) or Pomodoro (structured 25/5 intervals). Both contribute massively to growth.

🛡️

The Zen Block

While in Pomodoro mode, your "Distraction" domains are automatically blocked, redirecting you back to a calm focus page to stay on track.

🏷️

Smart Tagging

Sites fall into Growth (Study/Work), Neutral (Tools), or Distraction (Social). You can fully customize these in Settings.

📊

Visual History

Review your day with percentage breakdowns in the Tracker tab, or journal your journey in the Calendar.

🔒

100% Privacy

Every second tracked and every site visited stays entirely on your computer. We have no servers and no access to your data.

⚙️

Curating Your Digital Environment

Manage your domains in the Settings tab to define what counts as growth and what is a distraction.

Blocked

Sites that wilt your tree. These locations are automatically blocked during Pomodoro mode.

Neutral

Tools (like Search Engines) that you need for work but don't directly contribute to growth.

Growth

Sites where the magic happens. Every minute spent here feeds your paradise.

Pro Tip: Use the Category Toggle next to each list to quickly enable or disable entire groups of sites without deleting them.

Choosing Your Paradise

Your productivity space should inspire you. Switch between four unique environments in Settings.

🌴 CLASSIC

Oasis paradise

The original productivity companion. As your focus slips, the leaves physically droop and coconuts begin to fall to the sand one by one.

🌵 RESILIENT

Highland Desert

Changes from vibrant green to a dusty gray after 2 hours of inactivity. Hard work rewards you with a rare pink Oasis Jewel bloom at 90% productivity.

🌸 ZEN

Sakura Valley

A serene cherry tree that sheds its beauty. Inactivity causes petals to gather in a lush pink carpet at the base. Stay active to keep the canopy full!

🌙 TECH-ZEN

Midnight Willow

A bioluminescent willow. The neon vines grow longer as your productivity hits new heights, while fireflies dance around you at 60%+ focus.

💎

Zen Principle: Consistency

Building a paradise takes time. Your tree's health resets every 24 hours to give you a fresh start. If you step away from your keyboard, we'll automatically pause your timers so your data stays pure.
